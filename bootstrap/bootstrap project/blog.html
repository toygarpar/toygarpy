Salsabil Kur'an'ı Kerim Meali – Seeketh the way!

Proclaim ! Read ! in the name Of thy Lord and Cherisher, Who Created man out of A mere clot Of congealed blood. Proclaim ! And thy Lord Is Most Bountiful,— He Who taught The use of the Pen,— Taught man that Which he knew not.
